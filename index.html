About

I am a PhD student at SUTD, interested in natural language processing, interpretability, and AI safety. My prior work spans topic modeling with large language models, multimodal and meme datasets, bias analysis in pretrained models, and interpretability tooling for refusal behaviour and cross-modal systems.

For an up-to-date list of publications and citation metrics, see my Google Scholar profile.

Research interests

  • Natural language processing
  • Interpretability (including sparse autoencoders and activation steering)
  • AI safety — refusal, bias, and evaluation
  • Multimodal modeling (topics, memes, CLIP retrieval)

Outside research

I enjoy reading about spirituality, politics, history, religion, and dharma, and watching movies.
